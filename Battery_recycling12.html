Battery Recycling: Challenges and Potential

Introduction

Recycling of battery materials offers a crucial opportunity to support the growing demand for materials while addressing environmental concerns. This process has limitations but remains vital in reducing reliance on primary materials. Below is a summary of key points from recent research on battery recycling.
